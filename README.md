## Project Overview
This project focuses on developing a comprehensive digital marketing strategy for *Teagle*, a streetwear fashion brand operating in the Egyptian market.  

Teagle primarily sells online and participates in local bazaars, targeting Generation Z consumers with mid-range pricing (600–850 EGP).  

The project aims to strengthen brand awareness, improve market positioning, and build a structured performance-driven marketing system. The final output will include a complete marketing strategy supported by research, analysis, and execution planning.

---

## Project Objectives
- Increase brand awareness in the Egyptian streetwear market  
- Improve sales performance through structured digital campaigns  
- Reposition and strengthen Teagle’s brand identity among Gen Z consumers  
- Develop a measurable and scalable digital marketing system  

---

## Project Scope
- Market Research (Egyptian Streetwear Industry)  
- Competitor Analysis  
- SWOT Analysis  
- Content & Social Media Strategy  
- Paid Advertising Plan  
- Branding & Visual Identity Development  
- Performance Tracking Framework  

> **Final Deliverable:** A comprehensive Marketing Plan Document supported by a professional presentation summarizing the strategy, analysis, and execution roadmap.

---

## Project Plan (5 Weeks)

### Week 1 — Market Research & Industry Analysis
- Analyze Egyptian streetwear market trends  
- Identify customer behavior (Gen Z)  
- Study pricing benchmarks (600–850 EGP range)  

---

### Week 2 — Competitor & SWOT Analysis
- Identify key competitors in Egypt  
- Compare pricing, positioning, and engagement  
- Conduct SWOT analysis  
- Define market gaps and opportunities  

---

### Week 3 — Branding & Positioning
- Define brand positioning statement  
- Develop value proposition & USP  
- Build brand personality  
- Propose visual identity direction  

---

### Week 4 — Content & Advertising Strategy
- Define content pillars  
- Build monthly content framework  
- Design paid ads funnel (Awareness → Consideration → Conversion)  
- Set KPIs (Reach, CTR, Conversion Rate, ROAS)  

---

### Week 5 — Performance Framework & Final Presentation
- Define tracking system & performance metrics  
- Build reporting structure  
- Finalize marketing plan document  
- Prepare professional presentation
